About FreshBite

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FreshBite started with a simple mission: to connect food lovers with the best local restaurants and deliver delicious meals right to their doorstep. We believe that great food brings people together and makes every moment special.

Today, we partner with over 500 restaurants and have served more than 100,000 happy customers. Our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.
